Critical Making - Past

I thought digital work was not for me because it felt so far removed from the digital world. But after the selfie project, when a digital image translated to an activity that brought my grandmother to life for my family, I saw there can be a hazy boundary between the material and the digital. I loved this quote by Nick Sousanis: The visual provides expression where words fail. But what have we been missing? And what can be made visable when we work in a form that is not only about, but is also the thing itself" (59).
